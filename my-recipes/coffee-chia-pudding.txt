Title: Coffee Chia Pudding
Category: breakfast, chia pudding, no-bake
Rating: meh

Ingredients:
1 cup cottage cheese
1/3 cup brewed coffee or espresso, cooled
1–3 tbsp milk of choice (for blending)
1 tbsp pure maple syrup
1 tsp vanilla extract
3 tbsp chia seeds
cocoa powder, for topping

Instructions:

1. Add cottage cheese, coffee, maple syrup, vanilla and 1 tbsp milk to a blender. Blend until completely smooth, adding more milk if needed.
2. Pour into a bowl or jar and stir in chia seeds.
3. Let sit 5 minutes, stir again to prevent clumping.
4. Cover and refrigerate at least 3 hours or overnight until thick.
5. Dust with cocoa powder before serving.

Tips:

- Makes 2 servings: ~210 kcal, 17g protein, 17g carbs, 7g fat, 6g fiber per serving

My Notes:

- Ну такое себе — можно меньше чиа класть
- Разводила творог молоком на глаз и то получилось довольно густо и плотно
- Положила чайную ложку сиропа — совсем не сладко, можно смело класть столовую
